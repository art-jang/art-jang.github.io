About me

I am a last-year Ph.D. student at KAIST, advised by Professor Joon Son Chung. I earned my M.S. supervised by Professor In So Kweon at KAIST. I was a visiting student at VGG Group, University of Oxford, under the supervision of Professor Andrew Zisserman. My research aims to effectively train deep neural networks with multi-modality (vision, audio and text). Also, I have an interest in techniques related to sign language for helping deaf people.
